Report an Accident

Contact a GSA Accident Management Center (AMC) to report accidents, injuries, or fatalities associated with a GSA vehicle.

U.S. AMC (866) 400-0411
Fax (Atlanta Center): (855) 511-9159
Fax (Kansas City, MO Center): (855) 288-5171
Hours: 6:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. CST

If you need additional assistance and are located in the U.S. please email Nichole Saligner.

Drivers are responsible for reporting mishaps to an AMC. However, if a driver is unable to make the report, a supervisor then becomes responsible. When calling the AMC to report a crash or incident, please have the following information ready:

  • Any injuries or fatalities;
  • A brief description of crash, road, and weather conditions;
  • The date, time, and location of the crash;
  • Information on police response, if applicable;
  • The tag number;
  • Any towing and vehicle storage information;and,
  • If seatbelts were used and airbags deployed.

Submit the SF91 Motor Vehicle Accident Report [downloadable PDF, 240 KB] immediately. Do not exceed five business days after the time of the accident.

Accidents involving a fatality should be reported to the AMC within 24 hours.

Get Roadside Assistance

GSA Fleet Email: gsafleet@gsa.gov

Typical services may include:

  • Emergency Towing (to closest dealer from a legal roadway)
  • Flat Tire Changes (spare installed)
  • Fuel Delivery (emergency fuel delivered on the road)
  • Jump Starts (at home or on the road)
  • Lockout Service (keys locked inside vehicle)

Level of assistance may vary, depending on the agreement with the manufacturer. Roadside Assistance Contacts.

Report Vehicle Misuse

Citizens can report the misuses or reckless driving of a government vehicle at howsmydriving@gsa.gov

If the license plate does not begin with a "G", it is not owned by GSA. However, if you send a non-GSA license plate to howsmydriving@gsa.gov, the report will be forwarded to the appropriate agency.

Include:

  • Date, time, and location of alleged misuse;
  • Activity that is the cause of your concern;
  • License Plate (GXX-XXXXX for GSA owned vehicles);
  • Your contact information.

Optional but helpful:

  • Description of vehicle (color, vehicle type, make/model, etc.)
  • Description of driver and passengers
