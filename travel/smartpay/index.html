State Tax Exemptions (SmartPay)

Centrally Billed Accounts (CBAs) are exempt from state taxes in EVERY state. However, certain states require forms for CBA purchase cards and CBA travel cards.

Individually Billed Accounts (IBAs) are not exempt in all states. Certain states where IBA cards are exempt require forms.

Please select your state/US territory of interest below to see the exemption status and download the appropriate form, if required.

Not sure if your travel card is CBA or IBA? Check the SmartPay website (this site is not fully optimized for mobile).
