Regulations

GSA implements government-wide regulations that help agencies conduct business. Links below take you to GSA regulation sites that are not optimized for mobile use.

  • Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)The FAR is for use by executive agencies in acquiring goods and services.
  • Federal Management Regulation (FMR)The FMR is the successor regulation to the Federal Property Management Regulation (FPMR). It contains updated regulatory policies originally found in the FPMR.
  • Federal Travel Regulation (FTR)The FTR implements policies for travel by federal civilian employees and others authorized to travel at government expense.
  • General Services Acquisition Manual (GSAM)The GSAM consolidates GSA agency acquisition rules and guidance.
