National Capital Region (11)

More than 300,000 federal workers in the Washington, DC area count on the Region 11 staff to provide office space and related services. Region 11 manages approximately 95.6 million rentable square feet of space in 190 federally-owned buildings, including 70 historic buildings (seven are national landmarks), and 500 leased buildings.
