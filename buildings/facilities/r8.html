Rocky Mountain Region (8)

Region 8 provides work environments for nearly 50,000 federal workers in Colorado, Wyoming, Utah, Montana, South Dakota and North Dakota. GSA is the builder, developer, lessor, and manager of over 711 federally owned and leased properties totaling 20.2 million square feet within the region.
