Sustainable Facilities Tool

Sustainable Facilities Tool (SF Mobile) brings valuable sustainable building and workplace design guidance from the U.S. General Services Administration's (GSA) Sustainable Facilities Tool (http://www.sftool.gov). SF Mobile helps government and private-sector property managers identify sustainable practices and evaluate options for using them in their renovation projects.

It is also for facility managers, real estate professionals, project managers from government agencies, private sector developers, building occupants and owners.
