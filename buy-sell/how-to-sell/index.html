How to Sell to the Government

Eligible businesses have many ways to sell to the federal government through GSA. Start with these tools.

Understanding How GSA Buys
GSA offers professional services, equipment, supplies, telecommunications, and information technology from commercial businesses to government organizations and the military through acquisition solutions from its Federal Acquisition Service. Find out more about products and services offered through FAS. (links to full site)
Opportunities with the Public Buildings Service (PBS)
GSA also provides workplaces to the government by constructing, managing, and preserving federal buildings and by leasing and managing commercial real estate through its Public Buildings Service (PBS). Contact PBS Industry Relations (IRD) to understand what contract opportunities are available to businesses. Find out more about building-related opportunities. (links to full site)
Understanding GSA Multiple Award Schedules
The primary contract vehicle is the GSA Schedules, or Multiple Award Schedules, program. Learn more about contracts used by GSA. (links to full site)
