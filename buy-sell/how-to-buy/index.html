How to Buy from GSA

Federal, state, and in some cases local agencies can purchase products and services through GSA. Citizens can also buy from an online federal auction. Note: Links may take you to GSA's full site (not fully optimized for mobile).

Find Products and Services from GSA

Schedules
GSA provides long-term government contracts with easy access to more than 20 million commercial products and services. Place orders for commercial products and services at volume discount pricing with shorter procurement lead-times and lower administrative costs. Schedules >
GSA Global Supply™
Military and civilian customers worldwide can access approximately 400,000 tools, office supplies, computer products and other items. Simply select the items from the Global Supply site and place an order. GSA Global Supply >
Assisted Acquisition
Craft custom information technology and professional services programs for your agency. Work with certified contracting, project management, and financial management professionals to deliver value to your organization. Assisted Acquisition >
Technology Programs
Use GSA's pre-competed federal technology contracts to buy a full suite of information technology (IT) and telecommunications products, services, solutions, and access to highly qualified industry partners. Complex technology procurement made efficient and cost-effective. Technology Programs >
Fleet & Automotive
GSA Automotive is the mandatory source for the purchase of federal agency vehicles. GSA Fleet is a full-service, professional fleet management organization providing quality vehicles to federal customers. Fleet & Automotive >
Personal Property Management
Agencies: purchase items from other federal agencies or dispose of unneeded personal property. Personal Property Management >
GSAAuctions: FOR CITIZENS
Electronically bid on, and buy, a wide variety of government assets, from office furniture to airplanes. Competitive bidding, bids by proxy, instant feedback, online payment and online photos Includes Google map that shows the item's location. GSAAuctions > (links to a GSA special purpose website)
